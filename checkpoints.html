CHECKPOINTS

Home / checkpoints

Body-Paint Controls

Vehicle body parts are checked in detail.

Rear Layout Controls

Rear Assembly Checks are a general check of all under vehicle mechanical systems and layout.

Pre-Order Controls

Front Layout Controls are the general control of all mechanical systems and layout on the vehicle.

Fuel and Exhaust System Controls

The exhaust system is exposed to harsh conditions due to internal and external factors during use.

Internal Expertise/Electronic Controls

In this control unit, general controls of all mechanical and electronic systems that we control from inside or outside the vehicle are carried out.

DYNO Test

The dyno power test determines the maximum power that the vehicle engine can produce.

External Expertise Controls

External expertise checks are one of the frequently preferred auto expertise control points by our customers in the purchase and sale of used vehicles.

Engine Top Controls

Engine Top Controls: It is the section where the upper parts of the engine, which we encounter by opening the bonnet part of the vehicle, are controlled.

Engine and Drive Train Control

Engine and Driveline Checks: Our Engine and Drive Train Inspections are professionally inspected by our engine mechanics in a detailed and impartial manner.

Diagnostic Test (Brain Test)

With the diagnostic test, general controls of all electronic and electro-mechanical systems of the vehicle are carried out over the control units.

Debt/Damage/Changed/Km/Vehicle Detail Query

It allows you to get information about the damage and damage cost of the vehicle you intend to purchase, if any, retrospective damage and damage cost, changing parts inquiry and inspection-based mileage query. Results are obtained from SBM (Insurance Information and Surveillance Centre).

Brake/Suspension/Side Slip Test

With these tests performed at the measuring station, the Brake, Suspension and road holding ability of the vehicle is determined.

Carbon Leakage Test

Carbon Leakage Test enables the detection of leaks and cracks that may occur on the block and cylinder head surfaces of your vehicle with Special CO2 detection fluid.

Road Driving Test

The expertised vehicle is finally taken to the road driving test under the control of the technician.

1 Year-2500 km Paint Detection Warranty

With Dynomark Guarantee, your vehicle's body paint controls are under guarantee in your second-hand vehicle purchases and sales.

Mechanical Guarantee(Tur Assist)

Tur Assist engine mechanical warranty; It is a type of warranty that covers your vehicle against possible malfunctions and can be seen as a private health insurance of automobiles.
