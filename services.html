Brake Test

In the progressive brake test performed with the brake tester, the brake power and brake force distribution between the right and left wheels, the handling potential of the tire during braking, the degree of brake goodness, which expresses the maximum braking force that can be achieved with the unlocked wheel during braking, and the maximum.

Suspension Test

In the suspension test process, the suspension group of the vehicle to be tested is removed on the suspension tester with electric shock drive. Front and rear axles are treated separately and right-left imbalances, friction and roll rates are determined.

Engine Performance (Dyno) Test

The engine performance test of the vehicle is performed using a dynamometer device. The dynamometer is a device for measuring the power and torque of the engine in manual, automatic transmission front-wheel drive or rear-wheel drive vehicles.

Lateral Slip Test

The lateral slip test is a machine that tests the TOE angles (Tie Rod Adjustment) on the Front and Rear blades, which are important safe driving elements, in a very short time.

Diagnostic (OBD) Test

Auto diagnostic methods are of great importance in the problems you experience with your current vehicle. As vehicle technology develops and technological details increase, fault detection becomes more difficult than before.

Body Paint Appraisal

Paint and body appraisal is carried out by our expert and certified masters with Würth brand devices with the most sensitive measurement feature available in the world.
